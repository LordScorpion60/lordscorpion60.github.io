What is the Golf Simulator Toolkit and what does it do?:

GST is a tool designed for simulation golfers who want to enhance their game through advanced session and shot analysis. It extends beyond traditional simulation software by offering comprehensive data tracking, video synchronization, and editing capabilities.

What GST Allows You to Do

  • Merge all swing videos into a single video with shot data information overlayed on the bottom
    • Optional inclusion of a captured shot replay into the merged video
  • Create highlight reels by combining shot replays with shot data overlays when no swing videos exist

Key Features

  • Load FSX 2020 session data to analyze individual shots and their captured metrics
  • Control linked videos with synchronized play, pause, stop, and frame-by-frame navigation
  • Adjust playback speed down to 10% in 10% increments
  • Load and trim individual shot replays with custom timing offsets
  • Export merged videos with synchronized offsets for sharing with coaches and fellow players
  • Export individual shots combined with replay footage and accompanying data
  • Access demo content including sample shot data, swing videos, and replays to explore all export options

Currently, the following golf simulation software is supported:
